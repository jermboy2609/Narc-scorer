Relationship Screening Questionnaire

1. Does this person often dominate conversations and rarely show interest in what others have to say?

2. When you share your achievements or feelings, how does this person typically respond?

3. How often does this person lie or exaggerate the truth?

4. Does this person tend to blame others for their problems or shortcomings?

5. How does this person react when others are upset or in distress?

6. How quickly did this person express intense feelings or use terms like "soulmate" or "destiny"?

7. How does this person react to criticism or being challenged?

8. Does this person take advantage of others to get what they want?

9. How often does this person show a lack of remorse or guilt after hurting someone?

10. Does this person tend to manipulate situations or people for their benefit?

11. How does this person react when they are caught lying or doing something wrong?

12. Does this person need constant admiration or affirmation from others?

13. How does this person behave in relationships over time?

14. Does this person show empathy when others are suffering?

15. Has this person ever threatened you or others to get what they want?

16. How does this person react when they see someone else succeeding?

17. Does this person often have "falling outs" or conflicts with friends and family?

18. How does this person talk about their past relationships or friendships?

19. Does this person view themselves as superior to others?

20. How does this person respond when you set a boundary or say 'no' to something they want?

21. How does this person respond when you spend time with others or do things without them?

22. Does this person often talk about having a 'special' or 'unique' bond with you compared to others?

23. Does this person often seek admiration or praise from others, even for small accomplishments?

24. How does this person react when they see someone in distress or in need of help?

25. Does this person use personal information you’ve shared against you during arguments?

26. Does this person often try to control what you do, who you see, or where you go?

27. Does this person exhibit different sides depending on who they are with (e.g., charming in public, harsh in private)?

28. How does this person behave when things don't go their way?

29. Does this person often make you feel guilty for doing things without them?

30. How does this person react when they see someone else getting attention or recognition?
